A Beautiful Mind — Review

1. Your favourite dialogue and why?

"I need to believe that something extraordinary is possible." — This line struck a chord because it speaks to every person who is fighting their inner battles and still hopes for greatness.

2. Your personal review

A Beautiful Mind is a powerful and moving portrayal of John Nash’s journey through brilliance and mental illness. Russell Crowe’s performance is remarkable, showing both intellectual strength and emotional vulnerability. It’s a compelling story about love, support, and overcoming unimaginable odds.

3. Learning to take away from the movie

The importance of perseverance, empathy, and the power of relationships in helping us through difficult times. It reminded me that even in challenges, we can still achieve great things with support and inner strength.

4. Related concepts you've learned in the classroom

In psychology and management studies, we talk about mental health, teamwork, and problem-solving. The movie highlights how cognitive challenges can affect both professional and personal life — reinforcing the importance of empathy and communication in team environments.
